QMNF Foundation

Integer-only numerical framework that removes floating-point drift across every layer.

  • K-Elimination: deterministic RNS division at arbitrary scale.
  • CRTBigInt & Dual Codex: residue/magnitude coupling for precise tracking.
  • Fused Piggyback Division & Persistent Montgomery: conversion-free arithmetic cores.
  • Binary GCD, Padé, CORDIC, AGM: number-theoretic primitives in pure integers.

NINE65 Suite

Bootstrap-free homomorphic encryption variants optimized for speed and post-quantum security.

  • GSO-FHE & Shadow Entropy: bounded-noise encrypted computation.
  • MobiusInt, MQ-ReLU, Integer Softmax: residue-native neural primitives.
  • Cyclotomic Phase & Padé Engine: trig and transcendental support inside ciphertexts.
  • Encrypted Quantum (Sparse Grover): Grover-resilient keyspace design.
