About

Independent research in exact computation, post-quantum security, and deterministic systems.

Anthony Diaz

Founder

HackFate develops exact arithmetic systems for cryptography and computation. The core focus is eliminating the bootstrapping requirement in homomorphic encryption through integer-only methods.

All core algorithms are formally verified in Coq and Lean4. Benchmarks and a hands-on walkthrough are publicly available.

Technical Focus

  • Homomorphic encryption
  • Exact integer arithmetic
  • Formal verification
  • RNS-based computation
